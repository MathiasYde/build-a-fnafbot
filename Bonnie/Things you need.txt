Things you can need:

*Paperbag Clips (Just buy 100 of them)
*16/18mm PVC Pipes (2 meters should be enough)
*70mm transparent ornaments x2
*80mm transparent ornaments 
*At least 100x70 cm foamboard (About that size) (Three foamboards should be enough)

Tools you need:

*X-Acto knife (Just something to cut the foamboard out with)
*Hot glue (gun) (To put two parts together)
